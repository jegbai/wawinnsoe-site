About Us

Wawin National High School is one of the six government-run national high schools in Papua New Guinea, which are also known as "Schools of Excellence". The coeducational school caters for Grades 11 and 12 only, and is a boarding school

Vision & Mission

Our Vision: "ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE & GOOD CITIZENSHIP"

Mission Statement:"Wawin National School of Excellence stands united in its efforts to create a holistic environment that imparts quality Knowledge, Skills, Attitudes and Values with diligence and efficiency to produce intelligent, patriotic, culturally tolerant and harmonious students that have reverence to God, resepect for one-self, others and responsibility to actions for the empowerment of themselves and nation building"
